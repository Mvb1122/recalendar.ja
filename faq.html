FAQ

  • It's slow to generate the full calendar It's a trade-off - all the work is done in your browser (your data is safe with you!), but the speed depends on your device. Make sure your browser is up-to-date and maybe close some tabs ;) You most likely will only need to generate the full calendar once a year, so I think it's a fair trade-off.
  • My language is not supported Please check on GitHub if there's an open issue tracking your language. Upvote it to record your interest. Or better yet - help translate it!
  • Where do I report issues? Please do it on the GitHub project.
